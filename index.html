About Me

I am a graduate student in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Princeton University under the supervision of Dr. Dan Steingart.

Before joining Princeton, I received my BS and MS in the Department of Chemical Engineering, Stanford University in 2016. I researched nanomaterials for energy storage catalysts during my undergraduate and silicon-graphite anodes for next gen lithium-ion batteries for my Master's.

Research Interests

My research area is in next generation batteries. My previous work has focused on nanomaterials for high performance catalysts and silicon-graphite anodes for lithium ion batteries. My current research topic is lithium purity effect on dendrite growth for lithium metal anodes.
